Cipher Creator

Do you need a cipher for a TTRPG or other game? Just enter the word or phrase you would like put into a cipher. You will receive three ciphers. Please note the scramble works better on longer words and phrases and each cipher will not return special characters or numbers. Click the links to be taken to a wikipedia page for more info on each cipher.

  • Transposition Cipher
  • Transposition Cipher is just a fancy name for a word scramble. To make your scramble easier let your players know how many words long the answer phrase is.
